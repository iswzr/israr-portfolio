Four dashboards built for real business use cases:

- Construction KPIs: project timelines, budget variance, contractor performance
- HR Analytics: headcount trends, attrition rates, hiring funnel, department costs
- E-commerce Performance: sales by channel, product performance, customer LTV
- Emergency Room Operations: patient wait times, bed occupancy, staff utilization

Each dashboard features drill-through navigation, dynamic slicers, time intelligence (MTD, QTD, YTD), and mobile-optimized layouts.
